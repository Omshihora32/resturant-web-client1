Craftsmanship & Tradition

Born in Naples, Crafted with Purity

Forno Rosso was founded on a simple mission: to create world-class, authentic Italian artisan pizza while remaining 100% pure vegetarian.

Our master pizzaiolos train in traditional Neapolitan dough stretch techniques, pairing century-old fermentation secrets with farm-fresh organic vegetables and handcrafted tomato sauces.

2011 — Naples Foundations

First Stone Oven Fired

Started with a single volcanic stone oven and a commitment to 100% vegetarian artisan dough.

2015 — Sourdough Perfection

48-Hour Natural Fermentation

Mastered our signature sourdough technique providing a light, crisp crust that is easy to digest.

2019 — Flagship Kitchen

Pure Veg Dining Hall
